Marrakech

"Between the ochre walls of the Medina and the snow-capped Atlas peaks, Marrakech breathes in a rhythm of ancient spice markets and modernist desert dreams."

The city is a kaleidoscope of sensory wonders. From the rhythmic chanting of the call to prayer to the intricate zellige tiles hidden within quiet riads, Marrakech offers a journey through time where every corner reveals a story carved in cedar wood and stone.

The Grand Palaces & Sites

Koutoubia Mosque

The city's spiritual anchor. Its 12th-century minaret has served as the architectural model for the Giralda in Seville.
