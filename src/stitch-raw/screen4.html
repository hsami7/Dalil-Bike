Villa d'Este

star 4.9 (128 Reviews)
location_on Cernobbio, Lake Como

Set on the shores of Lake Como, this 16th-century former princely residence is the epitome of classical Italian luxury. Surrounded by 25 acres of enchanting parkland, it offers a cinematic retreat where history and modern opulence seamlessly intertwine.

The Heritage

Originally built in 1568 as the summer residence of Cardinal Tolomeo Gallio, the estate was transformed into a luxury hotel in 1873. It has since hosted royalty, aristocrats, and artists seeking the profound tranquility of the lake.

The acclaimed floating pool, suspended directly over Lake Como, remains one of the architectural marvels of the property, offering an unparalleled vantage point of the surrounding Alps.
